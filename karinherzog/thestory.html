The Story

Karin Herzog is the worldwide leader in oxygen-based skincare. It is the only range formulated with stabilized active oxygen, which acts as a rocket booster, propelling essential nutrients the the collagen producing cells.

Their revolutionary skin care line was founded over 40 years go in Switzerland by nobel Institute honoree Dr. Paul Herzog. They are still run by the Herzog family today and all of their products are produced in their lab in Switzerland.

From Medical Research...

Dr. Herzog dedicated his entire medical career to the study of respiration and researching the powerful effects of oxygen on the body. He worked as a researcher for the renowned Karol- inska Institute in Stockhold and was part of the preeminent team that developed the artificial respirator. His achievements culminated in 1964, when he received the title of Dr. Honoris Causa from the prestigious Nobel Assembly.

After 30 years of relentless work, Dr. Herzog made the decision to retire. He and his wife, Karin, moved to the shores of the stunning Lake Geneva where they lived with their three daugh- ters Karina, Paulina, and Noelle.

While in retirement Dr. Herzog’s active mind would not leave him in peace. He had a strong belief that the powerful antiseptic properties of oxygen could be used to treat medical skin conditions. However, active oxygen is very volatile and scientists before him had tried, and failed, to stabilize it in a liquid base.

...To A Family Collaboration...

Karin encouraged her husband to set up a small laboratory in their house to research his theories. After eight years of dedicated testing, Dr. Herzog succeeded in his quest. In 1974, he was awarded the global patent for his breakthrough His research was solely dedicated to medicinal uses but as a professional esthetician, Karin quickly noticed a different benefit. To help her husband, she personally tested the emulsion during its development and was astonished by the results!

Her skin quality tramatically improved with tightened pores, reduced lines and a radiant complexion. She was sure the formula could help women all over the world to rediscover radiant, nourished and youthful looking skin.

... To Revolutionizing Beauty

Paul and Karin worked together to integrate his breakthrough technology in an anti-aging and restorative skincare line; Karin Herzog. Today Dr. Herzog’s proprietary oxygen formula has been used to develop a full facial and body skincare range that works to boost the skin’s own natural replenishment and recovery mechanisms to help revolutionize the skin from the inside, out.

They [and we here at SkinCare by Alana] invite you to discover the restoring and hydrating qualities of the Karin Herzog skincare philosophy today!
